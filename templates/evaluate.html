How Evaluation Works

The evaluation process:

  1. Upload sets of known "good" and "bad" cheque images
  2. The system runs detection on each image
  3. Results are compared against the known labels
  4. Performance metrics are calculated (accuracy, confusion matrix)

This helps verify how well the model performs and identify any issues with detection.
